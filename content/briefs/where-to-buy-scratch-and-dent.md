## Research Brief

### Primary Question
What types of stores sell scratch and dent appliances, and how do I find them near me?

### Target Reader
- First-time scratch and dent shoppers unsure where to look
- Budget-conscious consumers who only know about big box stores
- Homeowners planning a kitchen remodel who want discount options
- Online shoppers wondering if scratch and dent is available on the web

### Why This Matters
- **Selection varies wildly:** Outlet stores carry 10x the scratch and dent inventory of big box retailers
- **Price gaps between store types:** Independent dealers often beat big box by 15-25% on identical models
- **Local options exist everywhere:** Most metro areas have 3-5+ dedicated scratch and dent dealers
- **Online options are growing:** Several major retailers now ship scratch and dent nationwide

### Key Findings
- Manufacturer outlet stores (Whirlpool, GE, Samsung) offer the deepest discounts at 40-70% off retail with full manufacturer warranties
- Big box retailers (Home Depot, Lowe's) carry scratch and dent in-store as "open box" — savings typically 15-30% off
- Independent appliance dealers often have dedicated scratch and dent sections with room to negotiate
- Online retailers like AJ Madison, AppliancesConnection, and even Amazon Warehouse sell scratch and dent with detailed damage descriptions
- Habitat for Humanity ReStores carry donated and scratch and dent appliances at steep discounts, with proceeds supporting affordable housing
- Appliance liquidators buy truckloads from manufacturers and sell at 50-70% off, but selection is unpredictable

### Common Misconceptions
- "Only big box stores sell scratch and dent" — FALSE: Independent dealers, outlet stores, and liquidators often have better inventory and prices
- "You can't buy scratch and dent online" — FALSE: Multiple online retailers ship scratch and dent nationwide with damage photos and descriptions
- "All scratch and dent stores are sketchy" — FALSE: Manufacturer outlet stores are run by the brands themselves with full warranty support

### Scope Boundaries
This research explicitly does NOT cover:
- Specific store recommendations or endorsements
- Real-time inventory or current pricing at any retailer
- Used or refurbished appliance marketplaces (covered in scratch-and-dent-vs-refurbished)

### Risk & Caution Notes
- Always verify warranty terms regardless of store type — some liquidators sell "as-is"
- Online scratch and dent purchases carry shipping damage risk; confirm damage-in-transit policies before ordering
- "Scratch and dent" labeling is not standardized — inspect carefully regardless of where you buy
