## Research Brief

### Primary Question
Can you return scratch and dent appliances, and what should you know about return policies before buying?

### Target Reader
- First-time scratch and dent shoppers nervous about the purchase
- Budget-conscious buyers who want a safety net
- Anyone who has been burned by a no-return policy before

### Why This Matters
- Return policies on scratch and dent vary wildly between retailers
- Some stores sell scratch and dent as "all sales final" with no recourse
- Understanding your rights upfront prevents expensive regret

### Key Findings
- Big-box retailers like Lowe's and Home Depot generally allow returns on scratch and dent appliances within 48 hours of delivery, but policies vary by location and item condition
- Many independent scratch and dent stores sell on an "all sales final" basis, especially for deeply discounted items
- Manufacturer warranties still apply to scratch and dent appliances and cover functional defects regardless of the retailer's return policy
- Credit card purchase protection (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) can provide an additional layer of return protection for 90-120 days
- Some retailers offer exchange-only policies rather than full refunds on discounted appliances
- Getting the return policy in writing before purchase is the single most important step a buyer can take

### Common Misconceptions
- "Scratch and dent means no returns ever" - Many major retailers do accept returns; it depends on the store
- "The cosmetic damage voids the warranty" - Manufacturer warranties cover functional defects regardless of cosmetic condition
- "You have the same return window as new appliances" - Return windows for scratch and dent are often shorter or more restrictive

### Scope Boundaries
This research explicitly does NOT cover:
- Specific retailer return policy details (these change frequently)
- Legal advice on consumer protection claims
- Returning appliances bought from private sellers or auctions

### Risk & Caution Notes
- Always get the return policy in writing before completing a purchase
- Test the appliance immediately upon delivery to catch functional issues within the return window
- Keep all receipts, delivery paperwork, and warranty documentation
