## Full Outline

### 1. What to Look For

The three most important dishwasher specs are noise level (measured in dBA), wash system design, and drying performance. Third racks for silverware free up space in the lower baskets.

### 2. Best Budget Dishwasher

Budget dishwashers have improved dramatically. Models in the $500-700 range now offer stainless steel tubs, soil sensors, and noise levels under 50 dBA.

### 3. Quietest Dishwasher

For open-concept homes, noise matters. The quietest dishwashers operate at 38-42 dBA — quieter than a library. Bosch and Miele consistently produce the quietest models.

### 4. Best Overall Dishwasher

The best overall picks balance cleaning power, drying performance, noise, and reliability. AutoDose detergent dispensers and WiFi connectivity are becoming standard at the mid-range.

### 5. Most Reliable Brands

Yale's service data shows significant differences between brands. Some premium brands need repairs twice as often as their mid-range competitors.

## The Scratch & Dent Angle

Dishwashers are perfect scratch & dent candidates. Once installed, only the front panel is visible, so a dent on the side or top is completely hidden. Save 30-50% on the same models reviewed above by shopping scratch & dent.
