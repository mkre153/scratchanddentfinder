## Full Outline

### 1. Best Times to Buy

Appliance prices follow predictable cycles. New models launch in fall, which means last year's models get discounted. Holiday weekends bring promotional pricing but not always the deepest discounts.

### 2. Where to Find Deals

Big-box retailers, independent dealers, outlet stores, and scratch & dent shops all offer different advantages. Independent dealers often have more flexibility on pricing and better service.

### 3. Package Deal Strategies

Buying a full kitchen suite (refrigerator, range, dishwasher, microwave) as a package typically saves 10-20% compared to buying individually. Most brands offer package rebates.

### 4. Open-Box vs Scratch & Dent

Open-box items have been returned but may have hidden issues. Scratch & dent items are brand new with a known cosmetic flaw — typically a safer bet with a lower price.

### 5. Negotiation Tips

Most appliance stores have margin to negotiate, especially on floor models and discontinued items. Asking about price matching, delivery fees, and extended warranties can save hundreds.

Scratch & dent stores offer the deepest consistent discounts — 30-70% off retail on brand-new appliances. Unlike sale pricing that comes and goes, scratch & dent savings are available year-round. Browse our directory to find stores near you.
