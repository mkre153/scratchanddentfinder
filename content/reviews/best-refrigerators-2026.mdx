## Full Outline

### 1. Best Budget Refrigerator

Yale's top budget pick balances price and reliability. Look for models with adjustable shelving and energy-efficient compressors. Even budget refrigerators now come with ice makers and LED lighting.

### 2. Best French Door Refrigerator

French door models dominate the market for good reason — wide shelves, easy access, and bottom freezer drawers. Yale recommends models with dual evaporators to prevent odor transfer between compartments.

### 3. Best Counter-Depth Refrigerator

Counter-depth models sit flush with your cabinetry for a built-in look without the built-in price. They sacrifice some interior capacity but look significantly better in modern kitchens.

### 4. Best Side-by-Side Refrigerator

Side-by-side models work well in narrow kitchens where full-width doors would block traffic. Modern versions have improved the narrow freezer width that older models struggled with.

### 5. Most Reliable Brands

Yale tracks service data across 40,000+ appliances per year. Their reliability data shows some brands need significantly fewer repairs in the first year.
