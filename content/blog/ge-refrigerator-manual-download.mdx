## How to Find Your Model Number

Your GE refrigerator model number is located:

| Location | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Inside left wall | Near the top, on a white sticker |
| Behind vegetable drawers | On the interior wall |
| Back of refrigerator | On the exterior rating plate |
| Inside freezer door | On some models |

**Model number format:** Usually starts with letters followed by numbers (e.g., GSS25GSHSS, GFE26JSMSS)
