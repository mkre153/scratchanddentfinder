# Page: City Page

**Example:** `/scratch-and-dent-appliances/arizona/phoenix/`

## Goal (1 sentence)
Enable fast action by showing local stores and reducing buyer hesitation.

## Primary User Question
"Where can I go right now to buy a discounted appliance near me?"

---

## Required Sections (STRICT ORDER)

### 1. City Hero
**Purpose:** Immediate relevance

**Must include:**
- City + category headline
- One short supporting sentence
- Optional CTA: Call a Store

**Constraints:**
- Smaller than state hero
- No education here

---

### 2. Store Listings (City Only)
**Purpose:** Action

**Each store card must include:**
- Store name
- Address
- Phone (tap-to-call)
- Optional "View details"

**Constraints:**
- Listings visible above the fold
- No ads or promotions

---

### 3. Buyer Tips
**Purpose:** Reduce anxiety

**Must include (max 3 bullets):**
- Inspect cosmetic damage
- Ask about warranty
- Confirm delivery options

---

### 4. Local Context (Optional, Short)
**Purpose:** Light SEO signal

- City-specific phrasing only

---

### 5. Soft CTA
**Purpose:** Retention

**Options:**
- Get alerts when new stores are added
- Suggest a missing store

---

### 6. Nearby Cities (Optional, SEO-Only)
**Purpose:** Internal linking and crawl depth

**Must include:**
- Up to 12 nearby city links
- Plain text or simple link list

**Placement:**
- After Soft CTA
- Bottom of page only

**Constraints:**
- Visually de-emphasized
- No cards
- No descriptions
- Must not compete with primary content

---

## DO NOT
- Add long explanations
- Add filters
- Add state-level content
- Add newsletter popups

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Gate)
- [ ] Listings load immediately
- [ ] Phone links work on mobile
- [ ] No empty states without fallback copy
- [ ] No SEO regressions
